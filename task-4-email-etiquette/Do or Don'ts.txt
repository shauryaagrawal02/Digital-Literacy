✅To Do
1.Consider whether what you want to post is appropriate before you do so.
2.Be professional and courteous in every relationship you develop.
3.Be careful to not give out your private information, such as your phone number and/or home address.
4.Check to see if the information is accurate before posting so that you are not providing false information to the masses.
5.Use the privacy settings provided to restrict who can access your posts or information.

❌Not To Do
1.Don't share your private and/or financial information via the internet.
2.Don't post content that could be seen as offensive, abusive, or inappropriate towards anyone else.
3.Don't get into arguments with others or bully someone online using online bullying tactics.
4.Don't click on suspicious hyperlinks or open unknown file attachments.
5.Don't provide too much information about your family, friends, daily activities or your location.
